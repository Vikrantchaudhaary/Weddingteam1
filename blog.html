We Gifted This Family The Coolest Wedding Photoshoot - And The Pics Are Setting Major #WeddingGoals!

Weddings in India are slightly different from the ones abroad. In India, they truly are a joining of two families, rather than just two people. To celebrate this sentiment, Weddingz.in conducted a contest on their Instagram page, giving away one lucky couple and their family a free pre-wedding photoshoot! Through this photoshoot, we aimed at bringing the two families a little bit closer, to allow them to bond and relate to each other. Through fun activities such as a switching between parents (the groom's grandmother dressed the bride, the bride's mother dressed the groom), food (brunch along with cute pastries and cupcakes) and the feeling of romance in the air, the two families truly became one!
