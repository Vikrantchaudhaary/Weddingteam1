About Our Wedding Team

Wedding Team History

We are the leading wedding portal in the world but beyond that, we are a few romantics who help couples organise their wedding; a unique day to enjoy with family and friends that involves hours of preparation and work. We enjoy sharing experiences with a lot of brides and grooms, we get excited with their stories and help them with their wedding planning. We're able to do all this thanks to the collaboration of thousands of specialised companies with whom we grow.

Our Vision

“Wedding Team” visualizes itself as the first choice of every person when it comes to the planning of their big day.

Our Mission

At Blue Ribbon Weddings, we offer a boutique approach to wedding planning and design. We limit the number of weddings we direct, to provide the best quality of service and attention to each of our couples. We would be honored to walk with you as your confidant, negotiator, and wedding expert, effortlessly planning and fulfilling every unique detail. If entrusted with your special day, we will strive to incorporate your personality and love story to keep your union the focus of your celebration.
